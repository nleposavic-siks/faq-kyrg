    How do I make a deposit?

    Log in to our platform – AdmiralBet, then select the "Deposit" menu. Choose your preferred method and follow the described steps.

    How do I request a withdrawal?

    To request a withdrawal, log in to our platform – AdmiralBet, then select the "Withdrawal" menu. Choose your preferred withdrawal method and follow the described steps.

    Does my account need to be verified to request a withdrawal?

    Yes, your account must be fully verified to request a withdrawal.

    How long do deposit and withdrawals take?

    Deposits are usually credited instantly to the gaming account, although slight delays may occur with deposits made via Bank Transfer or Debit/Credit Card. Withdrawals take between 2 to 5 business days for processing and approval, while Bank Transfer withdrawals may take between 2 to 10 business days.

    What are the minimum and maximum amounts for deposits and withdrawals?

    Each method has individual limits for minimum and maximum deposits and withdrawals. All deposit limits are displayed once you select a specific deposit method. The same applies to withdrawals - you will see the available limits after choosing a withdrawal method.

    Why was my deposit/withdrawal declined?

    There may be several reasons for a declined transaction such as insufficient funds, incorrectly entered details, or imposed restrictions. For more detailed information, please contact the customer support team.

    Are there any fees for deposits and withdrawals?

    The organizer does not charge any fees for deposits or withdrawals. All transactions are free of fees on our side. Any fees that may apply will be charged by your bank.
