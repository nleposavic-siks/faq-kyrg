    How do I place a bet in the Sport section?

    To place a bet on our platform, go to the Sports section. Choose your preferred event and select the odds for the outcome you want to bet on â€” it will automatically appear in your bet slip. Next, enter the amount you wish to wager and click "Place Bet" to confirm.

    Why can't I place a bet on a specific event?

    The markets for certain events may be temporarily locked due to changes in the odds. Because of this, it might be temporarily unavailable to place a bet on that particular event.

    What is "Cash Out"?

    This feature allows you to settle your bet for a certain amount before the event you wagered on has finished. The Cash Out option may not always be available during the entire match, as its availability depends on factors such as changing odds or the progress of the event.

    What is a "Live Bet"?

    A Live Bet is a wager placed while the match is ongoing. Live betting is available for many sports such as football, basketball or tennis, offering you dynamic odds throughout the game.

    What types of bets can I place?

    You can place different types of bets, including single bets, multiple bets, system bets or live bets. You can explore all available betting options under each sport for more information.
