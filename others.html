    How can I reset my password?

    If you have forgotten your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" option located below the "Login" button. You'll need to enter your email address, and the code will be sent to you, which you can use to reset your password.

    Can I change the email address in my profile?

    Yes, you can change the email address linked to your gaming profile. To do so, you will need to contact our support center for assistance.

    What should I do if a game does not load?

    If you are experiencing issues accessing a game on our platform, clear the cached memory of your device. If the problem persists, try accessing the game from another browser or device. If the issue is still not resolved, contact our support center for further assistance.

    Is it possible for the funds in my account to disappear?

    Your account balance may change after you place a bet or request a withdrawal. If you suspect that someone else has access to your account, immediately change your password.

    Can I play demo versions of the games?

    Yes, you have the option to access the demo version of each game to explore it and learn the rules before playing with real funds. The demo button is located next to the standard play button.
